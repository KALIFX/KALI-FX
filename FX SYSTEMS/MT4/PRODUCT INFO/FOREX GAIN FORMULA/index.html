FOREX GAIN FORMULA

Trading Strategy Guide

Go Short (Sell)

  1. Entry Signal:
    • A red candle closes below the FGF_35SMA low line. (You will receive an alert)
    • Both FGF_BURDYS and FGF_FBURD indicators display a red bar.

Go Long (Buy)

  1. Entry Signal:
    • A blue candle closes above the FGF_35SMA high line. (You will receive an alert)
    • Both FGF_BURDYS and FGF_FBURD indicators display a blue bar.

Exit Rules

  • Exit Signal:
    • Close the trade when the indicators reverse direction.
    • A trailing stop can be applied, with the stop loss depending on the timeframe.
  • Stop Loss:
    • Place the stop loss at the previous low (for long trades) or high (for short trades).

Risk Management

  • Capital Protection:
    • Risk no more than 2% of your initial capital per trade.
