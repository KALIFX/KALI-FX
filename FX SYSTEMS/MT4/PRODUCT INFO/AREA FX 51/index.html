THE AREA FX 51 SYSTEM

The Area FX 51 system is very easy to use. No complicated setup or rules are needed. Simply follow the system's generated signals and place your trades. The chart displays two lines of text in the top right corner, indicating the current trend and trade entry points.

Long Trade Rules (Buy Trades)

Entry Signal: "Buy Signal: Enter With 3 Lots on CURRENCY PAIR at PRICE" (e.g., "Buy Signal: Enter With 3 Lots on EURUSD at 1.4332")

Stop Loss and Profit Target:

  • "Stop Loss Placed At 1.4210"
  • "Profit Target Placed At 1.4575" (for one lot; keep other two lots' targets open)

Trade Management:

  • If the stop loss is hit: "Stop Loss For Buy Signal On CURRENCY PAIR Hit" - Exit the trade.
  • If the first profit target is hit: "Profit Target For Buy Signal On CURRENCY PAIR Hit - Exit One Lot" - Close the first lot in profit and move the stop loss to break-even for the remaining two lots.
  • If prompted to exit one lot: "Exit One Lot For Buy Trade On CURRENCY PAIR at Price" - Exit the second lot, keep the third lot running.
  • If prompted to exit all remaining lots: "Exit All Remaining Lots For Buy Trade On CURRENCY PAIR at Price" - Exit the third lot.

Alternative Scenarios:

  • If prompted to exit one lot before the first profit target is hit: "Exit One Lot For Buy Trade On CURRENCY PAIR at Price" - Exit one lot, keep the remaining two lots open.
  • If prompted to exit all lots before the first profit target is hit: "Exit All Remaining Lots For Buy Trade On CURRENCY PAIR at Price" - Exit all lots, regardless of profit or loss.
