NO.1 FOREX SYSTEM

Buy Rules:

  1. Buy when the 40-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA) is above the 80-period EMA.
  2. For intraday trading, trade between 06:00GMT and 16:00GMT. For better results, limit trading between 06:00GMT and 11:00GMT.
  3. Enter a buy position when the Commodity Channel Index (CCI) indicator crosses from below 0.0 to ABOVE 0.0. Wait for a break of the high of the candle that caused the CCI to cross the 0.0-line before entering.

Sell Rules:

  1. Sell when the 40-period EMA is below the 80-period EMA.
  2. For intraday trading, trade between 06:00GMT and 16:00GMT. For better results, limit trading between 06:00GMT and 11:00GMT.
  3. Enter a sell position when the CCI indicator crosses from above 0.0 to BELOW 0.0. Wait for a break of the low of the candle that caused the CCI to cross the 0.0-line before entering.
