DEEP PROFIT LAB

Summary:

Buy Rules (Going Long)

  • Green Price Border: Use the Green Price Border as a guide to identify buying opportunities.
  • MACD Pro: Look for the MACD Pro indicator to turn Green.
  • Trend Pro Indicator: Ensure the Trend Pro Indicator is Green and below the 0.15 threshold.
  • Mini_Max Line: Confirm the Mini_Max Line is ascending from below to up.
  • Trend Finder Indicator: Check that the Trend Finder Indicator shows a Major Upward Trend.
  • Exit Strategy: Be ready to exit when reversal signals emerge.

Sell Rules (Going Short)

  • Red Price Border: Observe the Price turning Red, indicating potential downward momentum.
  • MACD Pro: Watch for the MACD Pro indicator to turn Red, signaling caution.
  • Trend Pro Indicator: Ensure the Trend Pro Indicator is Red and above the 0.85 level, indicating a potential downward trajectory.
  • Mini_Max Line: Confirm the Mini_Max Line is descending from up to below.
  • Trend Finder Indicator: Check that the Trend Finder Indicator shows a Major Downward Trend.
  • Exit Strategy: Stay vigilant for reversal signals and be prepared to exit.
