PLT WINNER STRATEGY

Overview and Summary: PTL Winner Strategy

Strategy Purpose:

  • The PTL Winner Strategy is designed for intraday and swing trading, focusing on trend momentum.
  • It uses the PTL indicator filtered by trend-momentum indicators (Ozymandias and Premium Stochastic).
  • Strong signals are generated when both Ozymandias and PTL indicators align.

Setup:

  • Time Frame: 30 minutes or higher, with 4-hour being optimal.
  • Currency Pairs: Suitable for any pair, but performs best with synthetic pairs like EURGBP, EURJPY, AUDNZD, AUDCHF, as well as Indices and Gold.
  • Platform: MetaTrader 5

MetaTrader 5 Indicators:

  • Ozymandias (amplitude 2 periods)
  • NK Zig Zag arrow (60, 5, 3)
  • PTL (2)
  • Pivot Points Levels (delete at daily timeframe)
  • Premium Stochastic (9, 4)

Trading Rules:

Buy:
  • NK Zig Zag buy arrow appears.
  • Ozymandias shows a blue line.
  • PTL displays a yellow square below the candle.
Sell:
  • NK Zig Zag sell arrow appears.
  • Ozymandias shows a red line.
  • PTL displays a yellow square above the candle.
Additional Rules:
  • Maximum of two entries in the same direction.
Exit Strategy:
  • Place the initial stop loss at the previous swing high/low.
  • Aim to take profit at the Pivot Points levels.
