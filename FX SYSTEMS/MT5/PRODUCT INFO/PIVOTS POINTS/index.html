PIVOT POINTS

Overview and Summary: MetaTrader 5 Indicators with Pivot Points and Waddah Attar Explosion Strategy

Indicators:

  • Pivot Points Levels
  • Waddah Attar Explosion (Ensure to enable DLL dependencies for the indicator)

Trading Rules:

Reversal Strategy:
Buy:
  • Wait for the price to approach a Pivot Point level.
  • Look for an upward bounce and a green bar on the Waddah Attar Explosion indicator.
Sell:
  • Wait for the price to approach a Pivot Point level.
  • Look for a downward bounce and a red bar on the Waddah Attar Explosion indicator.
Continuation Strategy:
Buy:
  • Wait for the price to break and close above a Pivot Point level.
  • Ensure the Waddah Attar Explosion bar remains green.
Sell:
  • Wait for the price to break and close below a Pivot Point level.
  • Ensure the Waddah Attar Explosion bar remains red.

Advice:

  • Begin with one method (reversal or continuation) to simplify learning.
  • For exits:
    • Place the stop loss above/below the previous price swing.
    • Set a profit target near the next Pivot Point level or use a 1:1 stop-loss ratio.
  • Starting with the reversal strategy at extreme levels is recommended due to its reliability.
  • The D'Alembert strategy can be used for money management.
