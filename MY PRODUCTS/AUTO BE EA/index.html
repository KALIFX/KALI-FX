The AUTO BE is an expert advisor (EA) for MT4, developed by KALIFX. This powerful tool automatically moves the stop-loss level to breakeven as the market price approaches your take-profit target, helping you lock in profits while protecting against potential reversals.

Key Features:

  • Automatic Breakeven: Moves the stop-loss to breakeven as trades move towards take profit, ensuring profits are secured.
  • Automatic Stop-Loss & Take-Profit: Automatically sets stop-loss (SL) and take-profit (TP) levels for each trade according to your preferences.

Input Parameters:

  2. Use Breakeven: Enables the breakeven feature. When a trade reaches a specified profit level (in pips or distance towards take-profit), the stop-loss is moved to the breakeven point (trade's open price level). Set to true to enable this feature.
